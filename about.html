Umonpur - উমনপুর!

Umonpur is a small village but It has many things. There are many people living in this village. We have got primary School, big Mosque, Umonpur Madrasa, Umonpur Panjegana Jame Mosque, Umonpur Highway Filling Station, Agrani Bank and Umonpur Heath Complex is being build. We have two canal, Lake and Hawar. Overall it is a nice and Beautiful Village.
